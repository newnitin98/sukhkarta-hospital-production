# Content Strategy — Sukhkarta Hospital

## Brand Voice
- **Professional but accessible** — patients should feel informed, not overwhelmed
- **Trustworthy** — credentials and qualifications prominently stated
- **Local** — consistently reference Saoner, Nagpur district
- **Non-alarmist** — emergency content is factual, not fear-based

## Content Rules
1. Never claim "best hospital" — use "expert", "specialist", "experienced"
2. Always add medical disclaimer on service pages
3. Emergency note visible on all pages (via EmergencyBar and footer)
4. Do not reproduce Eka Care content verbatim — write original copy
5. Doctor bios are factual, credential-based, warm but professional
6. All phone numbers must be functional `tel:` links on mobile
7. All WhatsApp links must use `wa.me/` format with encoded pre-filled text

## Homepage Content Architecture
1. **Hero**: Hospital name + speciality + dual doctor credentials + 3 CTAs
2. **Trust Badges**: 6 key service categories (scannable)
3. **Doctors**: Two card profiles with bio excerpt, awards, CTA
4. **Ortho Section**: 6 service tiles + Dr. Gunjan credentials panel
5. **Cardiac Section**: Highlighted (TMT/ECG/Screening) + 6 more service tiles
6. **TMT Highlight**: Dedicated callout section (high SEO value)
7. **Facilities**: 8 facility cards
8. **Why Choose Us**: 6 differentiators
9. **Appointment Process**: 4-step journey
10. **Location**: Map placeholder + contact details
11. **FAQs**: 10 questions accordion

## Page-Level Content Notes

### Doctor Profile Pages
- Bio: 3–4 sentences, credential-focused, patient-friendly
- Education: chronological (MBBS → Fellowship)
- Awards: verified only, listed with event and year
- Conditions: bullet list (scannable)
- Registrations: show for trust/transparency
- Photo note: placeholder included, actual photo needed

### Service Pages
- H1: "[Service] in [City]" format (local SEO)
- Body: what it is, who needs it, what to expect
- Disclaimer: specific to service
- Form: embedded on every service page

### TMT Page
- Has the most SEO potential — most expansive content
- Includes: what is TMT, who needs it, how it works step-by-step
- Keywords: "TMT test Saoner", "treadmill test Nagpur"

## Image Strategy
- Brochure and doctor card images used in gallery
- All other sections use SVG icons and gradient design
- Doctor profile photos: placeholders noted, to be replaced
- Gallery section: placeholders labeled with what photo should go where
